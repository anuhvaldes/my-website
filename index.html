Our Story

Who We Are

Grace Community Church has been a beacon of hope in our city for over 20 years. We are a welcoming family of believers committed to living out the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our mission is simple: Love God, Love People, and Make Disciples. We believe every person has a God-given purpose and we are here to help you discover yours.

  • Bible-centered teaching every Sunday
  • Vibrant youth & children's ministry
  • Active community outreach programs
  • Welcoming to all backgrounds & ages

Our Ministries

There is a place for everyone here. Find your community and grow in faith together.

Children's Ministry

A safe and fun environment where children aged 3–12 discover God's love through worship, Bible stories and creative activities.

Youth Ministry

Empowering teenagers to build a strong faith foundation through fellowship, mentorship and relevant Bible teaching.

Family & Marriage

Supporting couples and families with counseling, workshops and events to build healthy, God-centered homes.

Worship Team

Join our passionate team of musicians and singers who lead our congregation into the presence of God every Sunday.

Outreach & Missions

We actively serve the poor and vulnerable in our community through food programs, education support and medical missions.

Bible Study Groups

Small group meetings every Wednesday for deeper study of God's word in an intimate and encouraging setting.
